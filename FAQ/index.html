FAQ

  1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

    2. Login here: https://hz.mynetpay.nl/Login

  2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

    3. Go to the printer, show your card, then select Scan as an option. The result will be mailed to your HZ email.

  3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

    4. Here: https://webshop.hz.nl/WebshopApp/default.aspx?menu=082076044027019251066025111065201099237062130097

  4. How can you book a project space?

    5. Go the Selfservice portal, then click Reservations.

  5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

    6. Park your car in the parking garage next to the judicial building.
