FAQ

  1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

    2. Login here: MyNetPay.nl. You can find it manually by Googling ''HZ Mynetpay''. Then, top up your balance if needed (5â‚¬ minimum) and click ''Web Print''. Follow the instructions and bring your student card to authorise at your nearest HZ printer.

  2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

    3. Go to the printer, show your card, then select Scan as an option. The result will be mailed to your HZ email. Scanning is free.

  3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

    4. Here: Webshop HZ. You can find it manually in your MyHZ portal, or add it there otherwise.

  4. How can you book a project space?

    5. Go the Selfservice portal in your MyHZ portal, then click Reservations. If it is not there, you can add it to the menu.

  5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

    6. Park your car in the parking garage between the judicial building and the library. Make sure to visit the HZ front office to request a free parking ticket before 16:00! Tip: just collect it as you arrive at school, so you don't forget during a long study session.
