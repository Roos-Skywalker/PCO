My favourite part

My first attempt at the novel Thunder was a failure, as all first projects tend to do, but I have learned much, and will continue to do so.

Motivation

Motivation is about setting realistic goals. Persistence is about breaking the goal down into objectives that can be easily achieved in the short term.

I found that a measured approuch of working out a MVP (Minimally Viable Product) is the single most important task. To dedicate some time on seperating the essentials from the nice to knows.

Whereas adding personality and flair is what keeps a project fun. If I can get away with it; there will be an easter egg somewhere. Always.

Beyond fun and efficiency, being able to help others or do ethical work truly elecates a project from fun to pride. Succeeding feels good, succeeding together multiplies that.
